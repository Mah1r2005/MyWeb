About me


Hello guys I am Ahmed Ashhab. I am a Bangladeshi globetrotter and blogger. I am 15 and currently studying in 10th grade in BAF Shaheen College Dhaka . I visit different countries and create blogs about country's history, food, culture and more.


I have visited India , Pakistan , Japan , United Arab Emirate , Sri Lanka , Saudi Arabia , Kuwait , Malaysia , Indonesia , Bhutan , Ireland and Russia so far. I am confident of visiting rest of the countries soon.


My Goal


MISSION: My dream is to travel the whole world at least once and learn about different cultures of different countries. And creating blogs about them so that others can also acquire a knowledge on them.
